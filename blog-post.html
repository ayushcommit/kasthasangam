The Impact of Retrograde Mercury on Your Career This Month

Mercury retrograde is a phenomenon that widely sparks dread, often blamed for delayed emails, tech glitches, and miscommunications. However, when we view it through the lens of pure Vedic Astrology, it reveals an opportunity rather than a curse.

1. The Re-evaluation Phase

During its retrograde phase, Mercury is essentially retracing its steps. In matters of career, this is the universe asking you to hit the pause button. Have you been rushing into a new project? This is the time to review your strategy, proofread your proposals, and reconsider past offers you might have dismissed prematurely.

2. Reconnecting with Old Colleagues

One of the hidden blessings of this transit is the revival of past connections. Don't be surprised if an ex-boss or an old client reaches out. These "retrograde returns" can lead to highly beneficial career advancements once Mercury stations direct.

"The stars impel, they do not compel. Retrograde is a time of review, not ruin."

What You Should Avoid

  • Signing major, life-altering contracts without reading the fine print twice.
  • Launching completely new, untested career projects.
  • Having ego-driven arguments with superiors.
