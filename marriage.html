Why is Kundli Matching Important?

In Vedic tradition, marriage is considered a sacred union of two souls. Kundli matching, or Guna Milan, determines the astronomical compatibility of the bride and groom based on their birth stars (Nakshatras). It evaluates 36 Gunas to predict the harmony, longevity, and prosperity of the marital bond.

The 8 Kootas in Guna Milan

  • 1. Varna (Work Compatibility - 1 Point) Evaluates the ego compatibility between partners to ensure mutual respect.
  • 2. Vashya (Dominance - 2 Points) Checks the power dynamics and mutual attraction.
  • 3. Tara (Destiny - 3 Points) Ensures the longevity of the partner and mutual health benefits.
  • 4. Yoni (Intimacy - 4 Points) Measures physical compatibility, biological attraction, and emotional bonding.
